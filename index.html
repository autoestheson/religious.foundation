Optimal Grilled Cheese Recipe
  1. Cut 1 slice white bread into 2 halves to fit in the toaster
  2. Toast until golden brown (approx. 2-3 on the knob)
  3. Put lots of slices of the yellowest cheese on one half
  4. Put a bit of mayonnaise on the other half
  5. Put the two bread halves together
  6. Cover with olive oil
  7. Microwave 20 seconds
  8. Put it in the Panini Press at max. hot and squish it real hard till the cheese comes out the sides
Done!
