Have you control over that which is external to you?
No, for another may steal that which is external to you.
Have you control over your body?
No, for another may cause harm to your body; your body is external to you.
Have you control over your mind?
Yes, if you resist the whims of another (if you make yourself a heden-heathen); your mind is internal to you.
Is it possible for another to believe what you believe?
No, for your mind is internal to you, while their mind is external to you.
Is it possible for you to believe what you believe?
Is it possible for one to wade through the same river twice?
