My First Seminar

This past Thursday, my college held its first official seminar. I had in fact attended two seminars before this: one in Jack's Creek during a thunderstorm, and another as a practice seminar — but neither of these compare to The First Seminar.

For those unfamiliar (most, I would assume), the practice of the seminar is powerful. The long story is that a tutor asks a small group of students an opening question about a selected work, which then leads into an evening of discussion. The seminar is intended to expand the mind, and the question of "how does it do that?" is, itself, a very seminal question. I personally believe it's ghosts in the walls.

I plan one day to write more about seminars. I think they change people.
