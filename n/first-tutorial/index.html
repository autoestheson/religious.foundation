My First Tutorial

To add to the Seminar, I have now attended my first Tutorial. Tutorials, as opposed to Lectures, represent another fundamental of my college. The basic principle is that a Professor, as an authority figure Professing their ideas to students, will leave behind and shape their students. Instead, a Tutor only takes the role of a guide while leaving the responsibility of thought to the students, allowing them to shape themselves.

Today we discussed Euclid's first definitions and postulates. It was almost in a way a series of small Seminars, where each person asks questions and allows for others to complete their mind.

While not related to the Tutorial, this evening will be my second Seminar on books 7-12 of the Iliad.
